Evening Glam Makeup Tutorial

About This Look

Evening glam makeup is all about drama and sophistication. This bold look features full coverage, defined contours, smokey eyes, and statement lips. Perfect for parties, dates, or any special night out where you want to make a lasting impression.

Best For: Night events, parties, special occasions

Time Required: 45-60 minutes

Skill Level: Intermediate to Advanced

Steps to Follow

  • Base Preparation: Prime and prep skin for long-lasting evening makeup.
  • Full Coverage: Apply full-coverage foundation and concealer for a flawless base.
  • Contour & Highlight: Create dimension with contour, highlight, and dramatic blush.
  • Dramatic Eyes: Create a smokey eye look with dramatic lashes.
  • Bold Lips: Complete the look with a bold lip color and setting spray.

Products You'll Need

  • Long-wear primer
  • Full-coverage foundation
  • High-coverage concealer
  • Contour palette
  • Highlighter
  • Dramatic blush
  • Eyeshadow palette
  • False lashes
  • Bold lipstick
  • Setting spray
