Identify Your Skin Type

Choose your skin type below to discover personalized makeup recommendations

Oily Skin

Excess sebum production leading to shine and enlarged pores

Signs You Have It

  • Shiny T-zone (forehead, nose, chin) within hours of washing
  • Visible enlarged pores, especially on nose and cheeks
  • Prone to blackheads, whiteheads, and acne breakouts
  • Makeup tends to slide off or look patchy by midday

Best Products

  • Foundation: Oil-free, matte finish formulas
  • Primer: Mattifying, pore-minimizing types
  • Powder: Translucent setting powder for shine control
  • Skincare: Gel-based, non-comedogenic moisturizers
Pro Tip: Use blotting papers throughout the day instead of adding more powder to avoid cakey buildup.

Dry Skin

Lacks moisture and natural oils, resulting in flaky texture

Signs You Have It

  • Tight, uncomfortable feeling after cleansing
  • Flaky patches, especially around nose and forehead
  • Fine lines appear more prominent
  • Makeup looks patchy or emphasizes dry areas

Best Products

  • Foundation: Hydrating, dewy finish formulas
  • Primer: Illuminating, moisture-boosting primers
  • Powder: Avoid or use minimal setting powder
  • Skincare: Rich, creamy moisturizers with hyaluronic acid
Pro Tip: Mix a drop of facial oil with your foundation for an extra hydration boost and natural glow.

Sensitive Skin

Easily irritated and reactive to products and environment

Signs You Have It

  • Frequent redness, burning, or stinging sensations
  • Reactions to fragrances and harsh ingredients
  • Visible irritation from new products
  • Tendency toward rosacea or eczema

Best Products

  • Foundation: Mineral-based, fragrance-free formulas
  • Primer: Calming, hypoallergenic options
  • Powder: Talc-free, mineral powders
  • Skincare: Gentle, pH-balanced, unscented products
Pro Tip: Always patch test new products on your inner wrist or behind your ear 24 hours before full application.

Your Daily Skincare Routine

A proper skincare routine is the foundation for flawless makeup application

1

Cleanse

Remove dirt, oil, and makeup with a gentle cleanser suited to your skin type. Double cleanse in the evening for thorough removal.

2

Tone

Balance your skin's pH levels and prep for better product absorption. Choose alcohol-free toners for gentle care.

3

Treat

Apply targeted serums for specific concerns like hydration, brightening, or anti-aging. Let each layer absorb fully.

4

Moisturize

Lock in hydration and create a smooth canvas for makeup. Choose formulas that match your skin type's needs.

5

Protect

Never skip SPF during the day! Protect your skin from UV damage with broad-spectrum SPF 30 or higher.
