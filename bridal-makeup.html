Bridal Makeup Tutorial

About This Look

Bridal makeup combines elegance with long-lasting wear. This romantic look features flawless coverage, soft contouring, and waterproof products to ensure you look perfect from ceremony to reception. The focus is on timeless beauty that photographs beautifully.

Best For: Weddings, engagement photos, formal ceremonies

Time Required: 60-90 minutes

Skill Level: Advanced (consider a professional)

Steps to Follow

  • Skin Prep: Prepare skin with primer and long-lasting moisturizer.
  • Perfect Base: Apply waterproof foundation and concealer for all-day wear.
  • Soft Contour: Create subtle contour and add a bridal glow with highlighter.
  • Timeless Eyes: Create romantic eye makeup with waterproof products.
  • Lasting Finish: Apply long-lasting lip color and set with bridal setting spray.

Products You'll Need

  • Bridal primer
  • Waterproof foundation
  • Long-wear concealer
  • Setting powder
  • Soft contour palette
  • Bridal highlighter
  • Waterproof eyeshadow
  • Waterproof mascara
  • Long-wear lip color
  • Bridal setting spray
